# 3. Understanding Internal Locus of Control

## Question 3
### What is the Internal Locus of Control? What is the key point in the video?

Internal Locus of Control means believing that our actions, decisions, and efforts control our success and failures rather than luck or other people.

### Key Point of the Video
1. Take responsibility for your actions and results.
2. Focus on things you can control instead of blaming external situations.
3. Consistent effort and discipline help in staying motivated.
4. Personal growth starts when we stop making excuses.

# 4. How to Build a Growth Mindset

## Question 4
### What are the key points mentioned by speaker to build growth mindset?

1. Believe that abilities can improve with effort.
2. Accept challenges positively.
3. Learn from criticism and feedback.
4. Do not fear failures or mistakes.
5. Practice consistently.
6. Replace negative thoughts with positive thinking.
7. Focus on progress instead of perfection.
8. Be patient during the learning process.
9. Develop curiosity and willingness to learn.
10. Celebrate effort and improvement.
